About

Wilson Johne writes small-form, cinematic music that lives between ambient and contemporary classical. Blending piano, soft electronic textures and a minimalist approach, he builds intimate pieces designed for quiet moments — study, reflection, or late-night thinking. His compositions aim not to fill space, but to give the listener room to breathe.
